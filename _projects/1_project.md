This project explores the intersection of **Stochastic Differential Equations (SDEs)** and **Deep Learning**. By utilizing the **Feynman-Kac formula**, we transform high-dimensional Partial Differential Equations (PDEs) into a stochastic framework, which is then solved using **Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs)**. This approach effectively mitigates the "Curse of Dimensionality" in traditional numerical methods.
