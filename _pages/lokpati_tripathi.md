**Dr. Lok Pati Tripathi** is an Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematics and Computer Science at the **Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Goa**. He earned his Ph.D. in Mathematics from **IIT Kanpur**.

His research expertise lies in the development and analysis of numerical methods for complex mathematical models. He is specifically interested in the intersection of numerical analysis and computational finance.

**Areas of Research:**
1. **Numerical Analysis:** Partial Differential Equations (PDEs), Stochastic Differential Equations (SDEs), and Variational Inequalities.
2. **Computational Finance:** Pricing option derivatives using deterministic and stochastic approaches.
