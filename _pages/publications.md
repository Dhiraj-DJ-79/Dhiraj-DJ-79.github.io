  <h2 class="mt-4" style="font-weight: 600; border-bottom: 1px solid #eee; padding-bottom: 10px;">Primary Research Directions</h2>
  <p class="mt-3">
    My doctoral research investigates the intersection of <strong>Stochastic Calculus</strong> and <strong>Scientific Machine Learning (SciML)</strong>. Key areas of investigation include:
  </p>
  
  <ul class="list-group list-group-flush mb-4">
    <li class="list-group-item bg-transparent">
      <strong>High-Dimensional PDEs:</strong> Developing scalable numerical solvers using the <strong>Feynman-Kac formula</strong> and Backward SDEs to overcome the curse of dimensionality.
    </li>
    <li class="list-group-item bg-transparent">
      <strong>Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs):</strong> Hybridizing stochastic differential equations with deep learning architectures for high-dimensional heat transfer problems.
    </li>
    <li class="list-group-item bg-transparent">
      <strong>Inverse Problems:</strong> Utilizing Dirichlet-to-Neumann (DtN) mapping for coefficient recovery in elliptic operators.
    </li>
  </ul>
