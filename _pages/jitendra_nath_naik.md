**Jitendra Nath Naik** is a Research Scholar at **IIT Goa**. His work addresses both the theoretical and computational challenges of Stochastic Partial Differential Equations (SPDEs) and Fractional Stochastic Partial Differential Equations.

* **Theoretical Analysis:** Investigating the well-posedness and regularity of solutions.
* **Numerical Methods:** Developing algorithms via **MATLAB** and **FreeFEM++**.
