Research initiatives, pre-prints, and works in progress.

Current Status: I am currently in the research phase of my doctoral studies at IIT Goa. My work focuses on bridging Stochastic Analysis with Scientific Machine Learning. Manuscripts and pre-prints are currently in preparation.

Primary Research Directions

My doctoral research investigates the intersection of Stochastic Calculus and Scientific Machine Learning (SciML). Key areas of investigation include:

  • High-Dimensional PDEs: Developing scalable numerical solvers using the Feynman-Kac formula and Backward SDEs to overcome the curse of dimensionality.
  • Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs): Hybridizing stochastic differential equations with deep learning architectures for high-dimensional heat transfer problems.
  • Inverse Problems: Utilizing Dirichlet-to-Neumann (DtN) mapping for coefficient recovery in elliptic operators.
